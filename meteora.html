Meteora, Greece

Meteora is hands down one of the most impressive regions in Greece! Located on the northern side of Greece, Meteora is included in the World Heritage List. Dotted with towering rocks, it boasts a breathtaking landscape, creating an otherworldly beautiful scenery! According to studies, these rocks were formed 60 million years ago, when weathering and earthquakes gave them their current shape.

On the top of the rocks sit the infamous Meteora monasteries, most of them constructed during the Byzantine times. They are the gems of Meteora! Although only 6 of the monasteries operate, they form the second biggest monastic community in Greece, after that of Mount Athos in Halkidiki.

Being one of the most-visited places in the country, Meteora Greece receives millions of visitors every year. Unsurprisingly, one of the best things to do in Meteora is hiking in nature and climbing the towering pinnacles.
